Often, I find myself learning a lot in a short amount of time. This is almost always accompanied by the rush of learning something new, feeling as though I've tested my own abilities, and a lingering feeling that, like most dreams, I will very soon forget these magical adventures that meant so much to me. 

Which is why scientists _write things down_. An extraordinary concept that I wish to absorb into my own skillset. I present this blog as a proof-of-concept towards this goal.

My interests lie in **cybersecurity**, **incident response**, **digital forensics**, **vulnerability analysis**, **social engineering**, and **penetration testing**. Sometimes, electronics and vehicle repairs (and fires) are involved.
